Welcome to Fixerly

Your local community repair cafe, powered by neighbor volunteers who fix electronics and clothes for free. Join us every Saturday at the Public Library or the Neighborhood Coffee Shop.

Request a Repair

Fill out the form below to submit your repair request. Upload 2 photos and a description — our Master Fixers will review it in advance to ensure it's safe and feasible.

Prohibited Items

  • Microwaves and high-voltage appliances
  • Petrol tools and combustion engines
  • Items containing asbestos or lead-acid batteries
  • Safety equipment like car seats or helmets
  • Items still under manufacturer warranty
